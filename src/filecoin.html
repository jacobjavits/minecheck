Filecoin

Designed to store humanity’s most important information.

The Filecoin network achieves staggering economies of scale by allowing anyone to participate as a storage provider and monetize their open hard drive space.

Let's Mine

Filecoin Rewards

No useless proof-of-work here. Storing more files is directly related to winning more block rewards. The more storage you add, the more filecoin you’ll earn.

Earning Filecoin

Storage miners earn filecoin by storing data for clients, and computing cryptographic proofs to verify storage across time.

Block rewards

The probability of earning the block reward and transaction fees is proportional to the amount of storage the miner contributes to the Filecoin network, and not hashing power.

Filecoin Network

The Filecoin network is designed to reward participants at multiple levels — from large scale data centers to local entrepreneurs with mining rigs that cover the last mile.
